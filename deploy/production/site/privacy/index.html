Privacy Policy

This self-hosted Postiz deployment processes account and publishing data required to connect social channels and schedule content.

Data processed

The service may store profile identifiers, access tokens, refresh tokens, publishing metadata, uploaded media, and operational logs needed to run the workspace.

Purpose

Data is processed only to authenticate connected accounts, manage scheduled publishing, and operate the service securely.

Retention

Data is retained as long as required to operate the workspace, unless removed by the operator.

Third-party platforms

Connected platform data is also subject to the policies of the third-party providers, including TikTok.

Contact

For privacy questions related to this deployment, contact the operator of this workspace.
